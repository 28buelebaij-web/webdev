Must-Do's While in Layton, Utah:

-Layton Hills Mall

Your premier shopping & entertainment destination. Shop. Eat. Play. Connect.Regional shopping mall featuring more than 100 stores including Macy's, JCPenney and Sports Authority.

-The Rush Funplex

People say this amusement center offers a wide variety of fun activities, including go-karts, laser tag, bowling, mini golf, and a foam pit. They also highlight the friendly and helpful staff, and the clean and well-maintained facility.

-Adam's Canyon Trailhead

People say this hiking area offers a beautiful waterfall, scenic views, and a variety of terrains, including sandy switchbacks, rocky scrambles, and a shaded trail along a river. They highlight the trail is family-friendly, dog-friendly, and provides a good workout, with options for both short and longer hikes. They also like the well-maintained trailhead with amenities such as restrooms, drinking water, and a bicycle service station.

-Layton Aquarium & Wildlife

People say this aquarium offers a variety of animals, including fish, reptiles, and mammals, and provides opportunities for interactive experiences like feeding and petting. They highlight the fun and educational experience, and the good value for the price. They also like the friendly and knowledgeable staff.

My Personal Reccomendations:

1. Start with a hike at Adam's Canyon Lower Falls

2. Eat Lunch at Weller's Bistro

3. Spend your afternoon shopping at Layton Hills Mall

4. Eat dinner at The Garden Grille

5. End your day with a movie at the AMC Theater
